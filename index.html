Shang-Wen (Daniel) Li

Senior Applied Scientist, Amazon Web Services AI
Democratize machine learning and understanding for human communication


Email: shangwel [at] amazon [dot] com
Research focus: speech and language understanding, dialog management, conversational AI, and meta-learning.

I earned my PhD from MIT CSAIL in 2016, supervised by Prof. Victor Zue. I joined Amazon Alexa and Apple Siri since my graduation, and now I work at AWS AI as a senior applied scientist in conversational AI. I am always looking for talented interns and team members passionate about deep learning, algorithms, and coding.
